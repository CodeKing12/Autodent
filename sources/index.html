How it works

Let's get that Ride

Step 1

Place a Bid Or Post an Auction

All prospective buyers are advised to preview, check the items and examine the conditions of each item before placing bids.

Once you have carefully reviewed items and you're interested


Sign In to an existing account or Register a new account to place bids through our website.


During the registration process, you will be required to provide the following details: -

  • Phone number
  • Address
  • A means of Identification such driver’s license number.

If you are having difficulty accessing our account registration or login page, please call for assistance.

Step 2

Win a Vehicle Or Recieve Bids for your Vehicle

The bidding will begin at a price appropriate to the seller's reserve.

If the highest bid meets the seller reserve then the bidder will be declared the winner of the item after the seller's approval.


Once we declare that an item is sold, we cannot reopen the bidding.


An item(s) can be withdrawn for lack of inappropriate bids. If an item is withdrawn from the auction it will be offered again only by the company’s discretion.

With just a few clicks, you are ready to go
